Classic Tiramisu

Why This Tiramisu?

This classic Italian dessert is creamy, delicious, and perfect for any occasion. It's a crowd favorite!

Ingredients

  • 6 eggs yolks
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 2/3 cup milk
  • 1 1/4 cups heavy cream
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 8 ounces mascarpone cheese
  • 1 cup strong brewed coffee, cooled
  • 2 tablespoons coffee liqueur
  • 24 ladyfingers
  • 2 tablespoons cocoa powder

Steps To Prepare Tiramisu

  • Step 1

    Whisk egg yolks and sugar together in a bowl until well combined.

  • Step 2

    Cook the egg mixture with milk over medium heat, stirring constantly, until it thickens. Let cool completely.

  • Step 3

    Beat heavy cream with vanilla extract until stiff peaks form.

  • Step 4

    Fold mascarpone cheese into the cooled egg mixture until smooth and creamy.

  • Step 5

    Prepare the coffee mixture by combining brewed coffee and coffee liqueur in a shallow dish.

  • Step 6

    Quickly dip ladyfingers into the coffee mixture and arrange them in a single layer in a serving dish.

  • Step 7

    Spread half of the mascarpone mixture evenly over the soaked ladyfingers.

  • Step 8

    Repeat the layers with the remaining ladyfingers and mascarpone mixture, finishing with the mascarpone layer.

  • Step 9

    Dust the top with cocoa powder and refrigerate for at least 4 hours before serving for the best flavor and texture.

Tips and Variations

For a richer flavor, use espresso instead of regular coffee. You can also add a sprinkle of grated chocolate on top.
